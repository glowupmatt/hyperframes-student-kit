NO.02COLOR

Three colors.
That's the rule.

Ink and cream do 95% of the work. The accent is loud on purpose — use it for one thing per frame, never as a background for body copy. If you find yourself reaching for a fourth color, you don't need a fourth color. You need to delete something.

NO.06VOICE &
TONE

Sound like
A friend.

The brand is one person who's a little tired, very curious, and genuinely amused by his own life. Write captions in that voice. Cut anything that sounds like a press release. If a sentence wouldn't survive being read out loud at a coffee shop, rewrite it.

✓ DO

  • write like you talk.
  • one joke per video.
  • admit when something is mid.
  • use lower-case in captions.
  • land the punchline on the last frame.

✗ DON'T

  • say "synergy", "leverage", or "unlock".
  • use three exclamation marks.
  • overpromise in the title.
  • forget the punchline.
  • shout at the audience.
